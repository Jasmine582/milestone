About Me

I am an inspiring minimalist who loves to travel and spend time with family and friends. My love languages are HTML, CSS, JavaScript and of course food!

After working in Human Resources for over a decade. I decided it was time for a change. When Covid-19 happened, it pushed me out of my comfort zone and into tech. I discovered front-end development on a whelm and I am happy I did! Building a website from scratch and troubleshooting an issue on my own have been really rewarding, because I am able to use my organization skills and keen eye for detail to write clean and efficient code.
